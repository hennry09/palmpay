We scale inclusive finance across emerging markets

PalmPay is a next-generation digital bank and fintech platform powering inclusive economic growth in Africa and Asia.

Across Africa and Asia, everyday life is going digital. A new generation is rising - connected, ambitious, and mobile-first. For them, the smartphone is more than a communication device; it's a school, a marketplace , and a bank branch in their pocket.

PalmPay is at the forefront of this transformation. In Nigeria, we've built one of the country's leading digital banks — trusted by tens of millions of people for payments, savings, credit, and rewards. In Tanzania and Bangladesh, we're beginning with smartphone device financing, giving first-time users access to the digital economy and a pathway into broader financial services.

Today, PalmPay serves 40 million users and 1 million merchants, powering over 15 million transactions every day. Whether through banking or device financing, our mission is the same: to expand access, unlock opportunity, and drive financial inclusion at scale.

PalmPay is live in Nigeria, Ghana, Tanzania, and Bangladesh, with more markets launching soon

Our Story

PalmPay is committed to unlocking access to modern financial services for everyone.

We launched in Nigeria in 2019 as a consumer payments app, making everyday transactions faster, easier, and more rewarding. Since then, PalmPay has grown into a comprehensive financial ecosystem serving individuals and businesses across multiple markets.

  • A trusted banking app spanning payments, savings, credit and rewards
  • Smartphone financing that opens access to digital finance for first-time users
  • Business tools that power growth for merchants and mobile money agents
  • An enterprise B2B platform simplifying payments and cross-border flows

    • From everyday payments to long-term financial growth, PalmPay delivers the tools that people and businesses need to thrive

    Driving inclusion through technology

    PalmPay takes a digital-first approach to financial services, making it possible to reach millions of underserved individuals and MSMEs. By leveraging smartphones, we scale access faster, at lower cost, and with fewer barriers than legacy systems.

    At the core is our proprietary technology: payment rails that deliver a 99.95% success rate and near-instant settlement, credit-scoring models that use alternative data to unlock financing for first-time borrowers, and AI-supported transaction monitoring and fraud detection that protects users in real time.

    Together, these systems make complex financial services seamless, secure, and accessible - powering inclusion at scale.

    Financial inclusion begins with phone ownership

    Smartphones are the gateway to the digital economy. PalmPay expands to new markets through smartphone device financing, selling smartphones on installment with the PalmPay app included. This turns the phone itself into the on-ramp to payments , credit, and long-term financial growth.

    Own it now, pay over time

    Affordable installment plans put smartphones within everyone’s reach

    Build credit as you go

    Repayments create a digital credit history, unlocking access to future loans
