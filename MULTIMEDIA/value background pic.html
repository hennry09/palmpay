Our Story
PalmPay is committed to unlocking access to modern financial services for everyone.  We launched in Nigeria in 2019 as a consumer payments app, making everyday transactions faster, easier, and more rewarding. Since then, PalmPay has grown into a comprehensive financial ecosystem serving individuals and businesses across multiple markets.
  • A trusted banking app spanning payments, savings, credit and rewards.
  • Smartphone financing that opens access to digital finance for first-time users
  • Business tools that power growth for merchants and mobile money agents
  • An enterprise B2B platform simplifying payments and cross-border flows
From everyday payments to long-term financial growth, PalmPay delivers the tools that people and businesses need to thrive

Driving inclusion through technology
PalmPay takes a digital-first approach to financial services, making it possible to reach millions of underserved individuals and MSMEs. By leveraging smartphones, we scale access faster, at lower cost, and with fewer barriers than legacy systems. At the core is our proprietary technology: payment rails that deliver a 99.95% success rate and near-instant settlement, credit-scoring models that use alternative data to unlock financing for first-time borrowers, and AI-supported transaction monitoring and fraud detection that protects users in real time. Together, these systems make complex financial services seamless, secure, and accessible -  powering inclusion at scale.
Financial inclusion begins with phone ownership
Smartphones are the gateway to the digital economy. PalmPay expands to new markets through smartphone device financing, selling smartphones on installment with the PalmPay app included.  This turns the phone itself into the on-ramp to payments, credit, and long-term financial growth.
